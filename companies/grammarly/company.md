# Grammarly / Superhuman — Berlin

## Role

**Software Engineer, Front-End** — Superhuman (Grammarly team, Berlin hub)

## What the product is

Superhuman Mail is a keyboard-first, speed-optimised email client. Grammarly is an AI writing assistant embedded across the web. Both demand pixel-perfect, accessible, high-performance front-end work.

## Company values (what behavioral questions test)

1. **Prioritize the Pond** — Act as an owner; team/company before self
2. **Grow Ideas from Anywhere** — Listen first; create space for all voices
3. **Think from First Principles** — Challenge assumptions; find the eigenquestion
4. **Craft Remarkable Delight** — Design with empathy; remove friction; be extraordinary
5. **Win with Grit** — Persevere through the hard middle; own outcomes
6. **(Super) Human-Centered** — AI amplifies humans; measure success by user value
